# 📊 Broaden Stores Financial Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents a financial analysis of Broaden Stores, focusing on sales performance, profitability, customer segments, and product insights.

The goal of this analysis is to transform raw data into meaningful insights that support strategic decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze sales, profit, and order trends
- Compare current year performance with prior year
- Identify high-performing regions, segments, and products
- Evaluate the impact of discounts on profitability

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 🔹 Revenue Growth
- Sales increased by **249%** (26.4M → 92.3M)
- Orders and profit also showed strong growth

### 🔹 Profitability
- Profit margin declined slightly (**14.68% → 14.10%**)
- High discounts (**57.8%**) are a major factor affecting margins

### 🔹 Regional Performance
- High order volume: Canada, USA
- Higher profitability: Germany, France

### 🔹 Segment Analysis
- Most profitable: Midmarket
- Highest contribution: Channel Partners
- Underperforming: Enterprise (negative margin)

### 🔹 Product Performance
- Top products: Paseo, VTT, Velo
- Opportunity to scale high-performing products

### 🔹 Sales Trends
- Monthly fluctuations suggest seasonality or promotional impact

---

## 💡 Recommendations
- Optimize discount strategy to improve margins
- Focus on high-profit regions
- Improve performance in the Enterprise segment
- Invest more in top-performing products
